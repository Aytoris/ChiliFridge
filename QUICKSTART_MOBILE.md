## 🚀 Quick Install (2 Minutes)

### **On Android (Chrome):**
1. Visit `https://Aytoris.github.io/ChiliFridge`
2. Tap menu (⋮) → "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
3. Tap "Install" or "Add"
4. Done! 🎉

### **On iOS (Safari):**
1. Visit `https://Aytoris.github.io/ChiliFridge`
2. Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
3. Tap "Add"
4. Done! 🎉
